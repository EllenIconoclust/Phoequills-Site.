About the Author

Welcome to Phoequills, the official storytelling space of Ellen Icon. I'm a passionate writer who believes in the transformative power of words. I craft stories that rise from darkness — tales that reflect the burning heart of human experience, redemption, and hope.

My journey began not with a pen, but with silence — a silence that demanded a voice. Through Shadow Love, The Dying Detective, and future releases, I bring those silent emotions into the light.

Phoequills is more than a name. It’s a belief: that even from ashes, a phoenix — or a writer — can rise again.

Featured Work

Shadow Love

A love story born in darkness, tested by fate, and held together by two souls who refused to give up. Inspired by true events, Shadow Love captures raw emotion and real connection.

The Dying Detective

A psychological mystery following a 21-year-old Indian detective in Paris. Rejected by his idol, he unravels a deadly web of lies while trying to prove himself in a world that won’t listen.

Weekly Update

 TITLE: TIMELESS LOVE  Our love is connected by hearts,   No longer two separate parts.   The moon, so purely true,   I’ll paint it a deeper blue for you.    The bond we sealed with a kiss,   A lifelong, unbroken bliss.   In hell, where betrayal and shadow stays,   It’s there our fearless love still plays.    Through storms that tore the skies apart,   You held the pieces of my heart.   In silence, in laughter, in endless days,   We danced through time’s uncertain maze.    So let the world forget our name,   We’ll burn as stars just the same.   For love like ours, both wild and true,   Lives on in light, and always in you.
